History

In the heart of the River Nile, nestled along the rushing waters of the River, Splash Adventures was born from a passion for adventure and a love for the great outdoors. Established in 2005 by two avid rafting enthusiasts, Jonh Abrem Jok and Rebeca Nyandut, the company began humbly with just a handful of rafts and a vision to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with others. With their expertise in outdoor recreation and their deep connection to the river, Jok and Nyandut quickly gained a reputation for providing exhilarating yet safe rafting experiences.
